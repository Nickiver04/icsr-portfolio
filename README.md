## Overview

This portfolio contains three Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) processed from real peer-reviewed PubMed publications. Each case is processed in full accordance with ICH E2A guidelines and international pharmacovigilance reporting standards, demonstrating competency in ICSR lifecycle management, MedDRA coding, adverse event assessment, and case narrative writing.

## Cases Included

**Case 1: Atorvastatin-Induced Rhabdomyolysis in a Patient with Liver Cirrhosis**

Source: Bhattarai S, Pradhan SR, Bhattarai S. Low-dose atorvastatin therapy induced rhabdomyolysis in a liver cirrhosis patient: a case report. Ann Med Surg (Lond). 2023;85(10):5232-5234. PMID: 37811121

**Case 2: Low-Dose Methotrexate Toxicity Causing Bone Marrow Suppression**

Source: Khuwaja S, Lyons M, Zulfiqar B. A Rare Case of Acute Methotrexate Toxicity Leading to Bone Marrow Suppression. Case Rep Med. 2024;2024:7693602. DOI: 10.1155/2024/7693602

**Case 3: Vancomycin-Induced DRESS Syndrome with Multi-Organ Involvement**

Source: Mallick D, Kaushik N, Goyal L, et al. A Rare Case of Vancomycin-Induced Drug Reaction With Eosinophilia and Systemic Symptoms (DRESS) Syndrome. Cureus. 2024;16(11):e73088. DOI: 10.7759/cureus.73088

## Standards Applied

ICH E2A | ICH E6(R3) | MedDRA Hierarchy | WHO-UMC Causality Categories
