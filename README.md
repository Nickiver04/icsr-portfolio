Overview
This portfolio demonstrates competency in Individual Case Safety Report (ICSR) processing, MedDRA coding, adverse event assessment, and case narrative writing. Three cases of varying clinical complexity are presented, processed in accordance with ICH E2A guidelines and international pharmacovigilance reporting standards.
Cases Included
Case 1: Drug-Drug Interaction - Warfarin + Fluconazole
A 68-year-old male on stable Warfarin therapy developing supratherapeutic anticoagulation and bleeding events following initiation of Fluconazole. Focus: DDI-driven causality assessment and CYP2C9 inhibition mechanism.
Case 2: Pediatric Hepatotoxicity - Ceftriaxone
A 7-year-old male developing drug-induced liver injury on day 3 of Ceftriaxone therapy for bacterial meningitis. Focus: seriousness assessment in pediatric population and Hy's Law threshold evaluation.
Case 3: Biologic-Induced Immune-Mediated Pneumonitis - Pembrolizumab
A 54-year-old female with metastatic NSCLC developing immune-mediated pneumonitis after cycle 4 of Pembrolizumab. Focus: immune checkpoint inhibitor toxicity, incomplete recovery, and follow-up flagging.
